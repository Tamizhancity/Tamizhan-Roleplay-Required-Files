## 📢 Rules

BASIC RULES OF TRP

Each Character Must Has It's Own Separate Story Line Along With A Separate Knowledge.

Multiple Characters Will Not Be Considered As Brother / Sister.

Knowledge Sharing By Saying (Brother / Sister ) Will Be Considered As Character Breaking.

You Are Not Allowed To Mix Character Information/money From One Character To Another To Gain Advantage Over Anything.

We Do Not Encourage Or Allow Character Breaking In Our City.

Strict Server Action Will Be Taken For Character Breaking.


## MAIN RULES

- 1. Vehicle Deathmatch (Vdm) : Is Strictly Prohibited ! Vdm Is The Act Of Intentionally Using A Vehicle To Kill Or Injure Other Person Or Where A Player Kills Another Person, Without Intent, By Reckless Performance !

- 2. Random Deathmatch (Rdm) : Is Strictly Prohibited ! Rdm Is The Act Of Killing/downing Somebody Without Engaging In Quality Roleplay. The Mentioned Roleplay Should Always Include Directly Initiating On Somebody’s Life Or Having Your Own Life Initiated On !

- 3. Fear Rp : This Means That You Value Your Life At All Times And Act Accordingly In Situations.if Your Life Is Threatned, You Should To Listen To Commands Given By The Person Holding The Gun Or Anyone Affiliated With That Person To Ensure Your Own Survival.

- 4. Exploiting Any Of These In-game Bugs And Using Them To Your Advantage Will Result In Counter Measures ! If You Discover A Bug That Can Be Exploited, You Are To Immediately Report It. 

- 5. Combat Logging During Any Roleplay Situation Is Considered Exploitation And Will Be Punished !
-  6.  Ban Evasion Is Not Allowed ! Ie, After Banned, You May Not Play On Other Accounts To Get Around The Ban Or Attempt Anything That Might Trick Admin Into Thinking You’re A Different Person.

- 7. You May Not Advertise Other Communities Through  (Roleplay). A Ban Would Be Issued !

- 8. While Reporting Someone, Kindly Make Sure You Have Sufficient Evidence.

- 9. Impersonating Any Government  Official (Police/ems) Is Not At All Allowed. Players Will Be Banned Without Warnings !

- 10. Speaking "Ooc" Through In Game Will Result In Taking Action.
